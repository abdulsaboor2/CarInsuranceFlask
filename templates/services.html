Our Services

Our comprehensive car insurance policy covers you against damage to your own vehicle and provides protection against third-party liability, theft, and vandalism. It includes benefits like roadside assistance, rental car reimbursement, and more.

Third-party liability insurance covers you against legal liability for damages caused to other people's property or injury/death to others in an accident. This is a basic requirement in many regions and helps you meet legal obligations.

Collision coverage pays for damage to your car resulting from a collision with another vehicle or object. This is particularly useful for newer vehicles or if you have a loan on your car.

Frequently Asked Questions

Comprehensive car insurance covers damage to your car from accidents, theft, fire, vandalism, and natural disasters. It also covers third-party liability.

To file a claim, contact our customer service team or use our online claim form. Provide necessary details such as policy number, date of incident, and a description of the event.

If your car is stolen, report it to the police immediately and obtain a crime reference number. Then, contact our customer service team to file a claim.
