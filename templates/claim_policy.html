Claims Policy

Filing a Claim

If you need to file a claim, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our claims department as soon as possible after the incident.
  2. Provide your policy number, details of the incident, and any other required information.
  3. Submit any necessary documents, such as police reports, photos, and repair estimates.
  4. Work with our claims adjuster to assess the damage and process your claim.

Claims Process

  • Resolution: Once the assessment is complete, we will work with you to repair or replace your vehicle and settle any other covered expenses.

Policy Coverage

Our car insurance policies cover a wide range of incidents, including:

  • Accidents
  • Theft
  • Vandalism
  • Natural disasters
  • Third-party liability

For detailed information about what is covered under your specific policy, please refer to your policy documents or contact our customer service team.
